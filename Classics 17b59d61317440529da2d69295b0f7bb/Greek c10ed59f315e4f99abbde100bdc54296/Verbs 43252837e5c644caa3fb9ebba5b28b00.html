Verbs

  • Basic properties and translations

    5 properties of Greek verb:

    Person1st, 2nd, 3rd, dual (2)
    NumberSingular, plural, dual (2)
    TensePresent, imperfect, future, aorist, perfect, pluperfect, future perfect (rare)
    VoiceActive, middle, passive
    MoodIndicative, imperative, subjunctive, optative, non-mood verb forms of infinitives & participles

  • Principal parts

    1st pp

    • Present active, middle/passive
    • Imperfect active, middle/passive

    2nd pp

    • Future active, middle (no passive)

    3rd pp

    • Aorist active, middle (no passive)

    4th pp

    • Perfect active
    • Pluperfect active

    • Perfect middle/passive
    • Pluperfect middle/passive
